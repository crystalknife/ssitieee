Rules and Regulations

  • The hackathon is for 8 hours.
  • Online registration will be closed on 31st December 2024.
  • Team should have at least 1 girl. Min Of 3 Members And Max Of 4Members, Also Should Provide Team Name.
  • Each member should provide their details at the time of registration.
  • An abstract in the format specified on the registration page should be submitted by the team.
  • A team is not allowed to submit solutions to multiple problems.
  • A team should have their team name!
  • Teams must present their ideas to jury members at 3 rounds and progress will be evaluated based on judging criteria.
  • Every team must disclose their team name as well as team leader's name (girl team leader) at the time of registration. No HARDWARE COMPONENTS will be provided.
  • The CREATATHON will begin on 3rd Jan 2025 from 9:00 AM and will end at 3rd Jan 2025, 6:00 PM. Internet facility will be provided for 8 hours.
  • Reporting time will be 8:30 AM.
  • All communication will be sent to your primary email submitted at the time of registration and in the group joined during registration.
  • Certificate of participation will be provided to all participants.
  • Clearly specify the email IDs and mobile numbers of all team members.
  • You are expected to come up with innovative ideas.
  • Lunch and snacks facility will be provided.
  • Abstract must be strictly in the form of a sample abstract.
